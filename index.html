IUse of A Scam Coin To Defraud (valobit)

Published: April 29, 2026

Public Notice: Valobit Rugpull Scam Alert (Europe & USA Victims) Hilton Consultancy issues this public notice to raise awareness regarding the reported Valobit rugpull scam, which has allegedly affected multiple individuals across Europe and the United States of America. Reports indicate that Valobit was promoted as a legitimate cryptocurrency-related platform and attracted users through promises of high returns, investment opportunities, and financial incentives. However, several victims have claimed that after depositing funds, they were unable to withdraw their assets, and communication channels associated with the platform became inactive or unresponsive. This type of scheme is commonly described as a rugpull, where the operators of a platform or token abruptly withdraw investor funds, shut down access, or abandon the project after gaining public trust. Hilton Consultancy advises the public to remain vigilant and cautious when dealing with cryptocurrency platforms that lack transparent registration, verifiable company ownership, regulatory compliance, or a clear legal framework. Investors are strongly advised to conduct thorough due diligence, verify licensing status, and avoid platforms that pressure users into urgent deposits or unrealistic profit promises. Victims of the alleged Valobit scam are encouraged to gather transaction evidence, screenshots, wallet addresses, communication records, and payment proofs, and to report the matter to relevant financial authorities and law enforcement agencies in their respective jurisdictions.

Public Notice: Misuse of Tax Identification in Fraud Schemes Hilton Consultancy issues this public notice to alert individuals and businesses about the increasing misuse of tax identification details and related documentation in fraudulent transactions.
